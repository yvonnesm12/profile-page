Yvonne S. Marquez

Independant reporter based in Brooklyn, NY.

Yvonne S. Marquez is an independent reporter from the Rio Grande Valley, a region along the Texas-Mexico border.

For nearly a decade, Yvonne has told stories important to LGBTQ communities. She has reported on the mental and emotional toll of anti-trans legislation on transgender youth in Texas and has profiled queer and trans people across the South.

Yvonne is currently attending the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism at CUNY, where she plans to specialize in audio reporting.

Follow her on Twitter @yvonnesm12.
